BUTZINGER

jacqueline julianna butzinger is a composer, sound artist & creative technologist working in the fields of electroacoustic and experimental music. She explores the intersection of sound and technology, creating multi-channel sound pieces, installations and performances, often utilizing self-built electronics and hybrid electro-acoustic instrumental setups.
